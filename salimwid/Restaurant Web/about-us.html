    ABOUT US

    SO, WHAT DOES GUSTO 101 MEAN?
    Gusto? Literally, "tasty" in Italian. The 101 is where to find us on Portland street. We're just a joint that serves nice food and wine.

    WHO'S THE OWNER?
    Gusto's owner is Janet Zuccarini, owner of Trattoria Nervosa in Yorkville since 1996. It is her passion for unique dining experiences that can be seen in every element of Gusto 101 - from the memorable food and wine to the cozy, yes cozy, industrial decor. She's a certified pizzaiola, seasoned traveler who seeks out the best ideas wherever she goes and ... someone you'd want to follow on Instagram.

    SO WHAT'S DIFFERENT?
    You'll find some unexpected global flavors hidden in our Italian classics. We're also a micro-winery with wine on tap. Kind of different, no? Best of all, we're affordable.

    Gusto101 offers a truly authentic Italian experience in a contemporary yet soulful environment in the heart of Toronto's Yorkville district.

    While we encompass over 8,000 Sq. Ft. over three different levels and seats 250, the space remains warm and inviting. Come join us for a memorable experience!
