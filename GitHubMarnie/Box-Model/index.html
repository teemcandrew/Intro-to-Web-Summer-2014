SPAIN

Spanish territory also includes the Balearic Islands in the Mediterranean, the Canary Islands in the Atlantic Ocean off the African coast, two exclaves in North Africa, Ceuta and Melilla, that border Morocco, and the islands and peñones (rocks) of Alborán, Chafarinas, Alhucemas, Vélez de la Gomera and Perejil. With an area of 505,992 km2 (195,365 sq mi), Spain is the second largest country in Western Europe and the European Union, and the fifth largest country in Europe.
