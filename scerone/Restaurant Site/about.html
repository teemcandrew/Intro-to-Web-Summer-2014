Who Are We?

Step into Dimmi Bar and Trattoria fine dining Italian restaurant where you find a hint of old Italy in the heart of trendy Yorkville in Toronto downtown. Serving well priced regional Italian dishes has been our specialty for over nine years and we continue to value your business. Whether you are dining on our street side patio or in the warmth of our fine dining room, or enjoying one of our famous Cazadores Margarita, with freshly squeezed lemon juice, orange juice, simple syrup and a splash of triple sec, it is our pleasure to see that you have the best possible dining experience.

Dimmi offers unique indulgences that play dual menu roles as quick, easy-to-eat lunches and satisfying dinners. Strong or subtle, Italian accents tempt pasta loving diners to look beyond the norm as our menu brings together excepcional flavours from Italy with a distinct concept.
